Online services

myCASA

Access myCASA for the following services:

  • Apply for an Aviation reference number (ARN)
  • Update your contact details
  • View and apply for subsequent issues of RPA operator's certificates (ReOCs)

CASA Self Service

ARN holders can use CASA Self Service for the following services:

  • Flight Test Management for flight test notifications
  • Viewing individual flight crew and maintenance licences and permissions
  • Submission of selected forms online

Medical Records System (MRS) - Examiners

A portal for DAMEs and other medical examiners.

Medical Record System (MRS) - Pilots/ATCs

Apply for an aviation medical certificate through MRS.

Online store

A large range of booklets, brochures, DVDs, posters and training kits are available from our online store.

AviationWorx

AviationWorx is CASA's online learning and registration system for the aviation industry.

Make a payment

Pay your licence, medical certificate, aircraft registration, AVID and ASIC fees online.

Flight Test Management (FTM)

Submit notifications of all flight tests for the issue of flight crew licences or ratings.

Defect Reporting System (DRS)

Report, search and view defect report summaries for aircraft or aircraft parts.

Manual authorising and assessment tool (MAAT)

Write and submit manuals or expositions for assessment.

Apps and resources

OnTrack

OnTrack is our visual flight rules (VFR) pre-flight planning tool.

Out-n-Back

Watch Out-n-Back, our ten part video series following a VFR flight in a Cessna 172 and G8 Airvan.

Can I pack that? - Dangerous goods app

Information about what you can and can't pack in your luggage.

Flight Safety Australia app

Read our bimonthly digital magazine on your smart phone or tablet.
